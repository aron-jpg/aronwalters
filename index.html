About me

I'm Aron Walters, a Leeds-based multidisciplinary designer working across 3D visualisation, interior and spatial design and creative direction. With a background in architectural visualisation, I help clients and collaborators bring ideas to life through concept-driven design and compelling digital visuals.

My practice is grounded in spatial thinking and strong technical skills, but I’m continually expanding into new areas, including interior design, creative coordination and digital project support. From photorealistic environments to stylised characters, I use design as a tool for communication and storytelling.

Whether it's visualising a future home, designing a space, or supporting a broader creative project, I approach each challenge with adaptability, curiosity, and a sharp visual instinct.

Based in West Yorkshire and open to projects across the UK and beyond, feel free to get in touch.
