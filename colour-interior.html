Colour Block Interior

2020 | Interior Design Concepts| Personal Project| 3DS Max/V-Ray

This was my first Blender scene, an exploration of maximalist interiors influenced by the bold home decor trends that gained popularity during the 2020 pandemic. Using colour theory, I crafted a harmonious palette with a warm pink base and complementary accents. To maintain balance, I kept the walls neutral while introducing vibrant contrasts through mouldings, skirting, and furniture, achieving a structured yet playful aesthetic. To enhance cohesion, I designed the wall patterns and rugs to echo the architectural elements, reinforcing the space’s bold and dynamic character.
