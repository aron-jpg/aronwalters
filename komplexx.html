Komplexx

2023 | Album Artwork Package| Prince Kongo| Blender

A commissioned album artwork package for artist Prince Kongo’s. Featuring original portraits in his likeness for the release of his 'Komplexx' EP and single 'Biloko'. They capture his bold presence through cinematic lighting and an urban backdrop. Inspired by Cynthia Kibirige’s photography, the portrait reflects the EP’s tone. The main challenge was achieving a natural afro-braided hairstyle, requiring a custom approach. Likeness was enhanced using a face-scanning plugin, and additional animated visualisers extended the project’s branding across platforms.
